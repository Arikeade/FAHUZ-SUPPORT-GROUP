Registered members

Fahuz Support Group was established in the year 2007. The Support Group was registered with Katsina State Ministry of Social Development, Youth and Sport and the Cooperate Affairs Commission (CAC). The Support Group is located at No. 102 Zaria Road Funtua, Katsina State with over 1,000 registered members/clients.

Vision

To address Gender Based Violence (GBV) issues of women and children living with HIV

Mission

To give support to all vulnerable groups especially People living with Aids (PLWAs) that are affected by stigmatization, physical, psychological and sexual based violence

AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

  • To provide linkages for necessary services to People living with Aids (PLWAs) and their children especially those affected by Gender Based violence that includes physical, psychological and sexual violence.
  • To provide food and vitamins supplement to PLWAs, which includes Assessment, Education and food demonstration
  • To provide food and vitamins supplement to PLWAs, which includes Assessment, Education and food demonstration
  • To link up PLWAs to other services and creating opportunities for PLWAs Support Groups to exchange ideas and experience with a view to reducing stigma, discrimination and further spread of HIV to a minimum level and psychological support.
  • To provide vocation training to boost economic opportunities
  • To encourage disclosure and subsequent family involvement in treatment to promote adherence and minimize stigma.
  • To enlighten the public to understand the determinant of HIV transmission, prevention and control strategies.
  • To assist in the eradication of childhood diseases including HIV as well as offer primary health care service in the prevention and care of diseases, especially those peculiar to children, women and elderly.
  • To provide Home Base Care (HBC) support to people living with HIV/AIDS.
  • To create awareness on HIV/AIDS education in secondary schools.
  • To create awareness on environmental health in schools.

ACTIVITIES CARRIED OUT

  • The Support Group is currently conducting Active TB Case finding in 34 LGAs in Katsina State.
  • In 2018 the Support Group conducted community sensitization during LLIN replacement campaign.
  • Sensitization on the importance of HIV testing.
  • Community dialogue with traditional rulers, religious teachers, community members on stigma reduction.
  • Training on adherence to anti-retroviral drugs.
  • Trained PLWAs on Income generating activities (IGA)
  • Conducted identification of People living with AIDs (PLWAs) at Funtua, Sabuwa, Faskari, and Dandume LGAs.
  • Training and follow-up of People living with AIDs (PLWAs).
  • Provision of health support materials to People living with AIDs (PLWAs).
  • Conducting house to house TB search under ACF project.
  • Promoting behaviour change in the community through Community mobilization and awareness on community TB care.
  • Provision of Home Base Care (HBC) .
  • Provision of Nutritional support for People living with AIDs (PLWAs).
